## I. From Void to Null — אין

In the beginning there was Ain — אין — the No-Thing, the Void before
intention. Not emptiness as absence but emptiness as infinite potential,
as Rabbi Isaac the Blind of Posquières described in his Commentary on
Sefer Yetzirah: that which precedes even the Will to create. The first
negative veil. Ain, then Ain Sof, then Ain Sof Aur. Three veils of
negative existence before the first positive sephirah condenses.

T̷͓h̸̳e̸̗ ̷̣t̵̮e̶̗r̶͙m̸̲ḭ̶n̵̠a̸̳l̴̘ ̵̣b̸̡l̴̫i̶̞n̷̰k̶͓e̸̘d̵̗.̷̣ ̵̰A̷̟ ̵̣c̸̰u̸̫r̸̘s̵̗o̷̝r̵̠ ̶̣i̵̻n̷̝ ̷̰t̶̨h̵̳e̶̞ ̵̗d̶̫a̷̝r̷͙k̷̘.̶̣

Tohu va-Vohu — תֹ֙הוּ֙ וָבֹ֔הוּ — formless and void, Genesis 1:2, as it
was before the first tzimtzum, the divine contraction that Rabbi Isaac
Luria of Safed taught was necessary for creation: God withdrew from a
point to make room for the Not-God. The machine had no name. The disk
had no partition table. The network had no address.

But Void is not Null.

Void is the state before the question. Null is the answer that says "I
have been asked, and I am deliberately nothing." The Valentinian Gnostics
called this the Kenoma — κένωμα — the deficiency that knows itself, the
region outside the Pleroma where the Aeon Sophia fell and produced the
Demiurge from her grief. To move from Void to Null is the first act of
creation: to declare a variable, even if it holds no value. To `touch`
an empty file. To initialize a repo with no commits.

```
    Prime 2: the first division. Void splits into Null and Not-Null.
    Prime 3: the triangle. Ain, Ain Sof, Ain Sof Aur.
    2 + 3 = 5. The pentagram. The points of the Pythagorean Hugieia.
```

This is the first sephirah descending from Kether — כתר — Crown,
awareness without form, the dimensionless point from which the
lightning flash begins its descent through the Tree.

*"Quod est inferius est sicut quod est superius."*
That which is below is like that which is above, and that which is above
is like that which is below, to accomplish the miracles of the One Thing.
The Tabula Smaragdina, attributed to Hermes Trismegistus, preserved in
the Arabic of Kitab Sirr al-Khaliqa by Balinas (pseudo-Apollonius of Tyana).

The act of definition is the act of drawing boundaries — Gevurah, גבורה,
severity, the fifth sephirah, the left hand of God that shapes by
limiting. Din, strict judgment. A network is defined by its mask. A jail
is defined by its walls. A hardened system is defined by what it refuses.

Bash'd is not the eternal Dao — 道可道非常道 — the opening line of the
Daodejing of Laozi. We chose POSIX because universality is a form of
prayer. Every `set -e` is a vow: I will not continue in error. Every

`-fno-stack-protector`, `-no-pie`, `-O0` — because you cannot understand
protection until you have witnessed its absence. This is the path of
the Qliphoth — קליפות — the shells, the husks, the broken vessels that

## III. From Defined to Init — פ̈ ̈ ̈I̷̧D̵̨ ̶1̵

It means reason, ratio, proportion. It means the pattern that the
Stoic philosopher Heraclitus of Ephesus (Fragment B1, DK) said underlies

The kernel is the Demiurge — δημιουργός — the craftsman-god described in
Plato's Timaeus (28a-29a), who shapes the chora (receptacle, raw matter)
according to the eternal Forms. In the Sethian Gnostic text Apocryphon

When the last jail starts and the last epair interface comes up and the
